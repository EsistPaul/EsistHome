  • 12.08.2021

    Realease of Soulike

    In August 2021 I participated in my first gamejam, the lowrezjam 2021. In the time of two weeks I developed my game "Monkeys and Puzzles". The game is a short puzzle game where you have to reach the end of the level with given resources. I reached 120th place out of 332 entries with a score of 3.630 / 5.


  • 14.07.2021

    Realease of Soulike

    Two months after the release of "Small Adventure" I released a new game. It's called Soulike and is a turn-based dungeon crawler with rogue-like elements. It was my biggest project so far. Even after the release I worked on it for some weeks and released some updates.


  • 10.05.2021

    Realease of Small Adventure

    After creating some unreleased games with Godot, I wanted to try something new. So I learned to use a new game development tool: "Pico-8". In just a few days I created and published my first game with pico-8, called "Small Adventure".


  • 27.06.2020

    Release of Catch the Time

    I have published the first game I have ever made on itch.io, called "Catch the Time". It's a little platform where you have to collect clocks to extend your lifetime. When the time runs out you lose. I created the game with the godot engine. I worked on the game for about 2 months until I published it.
